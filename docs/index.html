Our mission

Our mission is to be the one-stop shop for all your multi-omics data needs, providing lower costs for data extraction, processing, and analysis. We are dedicated to boosting quality standards and empowering researchers to make new discoveries by providing exceptional service and support.

The Process

We will help you collect and analyze multi-omics data through a 3 step process

  • Upload metadata : We will provide an excel template that will streamline metadata handling, ease experimental design and specify what data types you want extracted.
  • Barcode / ship samples : We will provide barcodes that provides shipping information to our service providers.
  • Download data + insights : We coordinate with our service providers to extract your multi-omics data. We will then apply our customized AI models to remove batch effects and provide interactive biological insights.
